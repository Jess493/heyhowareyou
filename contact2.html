Simple White Cake :)

  • Step 1: Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour a 9x9 inch pan or line a muffin pan with paper liners.
  • Step 2: In a bowl, cream 1 cup of white sugar and 1/2 a cup of butter together. Beat in 2 eggs one at a time, then stir in 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract. Combine 1 and 1/2 cups of flour and 1 and 3/4 teaspoons of baking powder to mixture and mix well.
  • Step 3: Finally pour in 1/2 cup of milk and mix all together. After done mixing together, pour or spoon batter into the pan.
  • Step 4: Bake for 30 to 40 minutes in the oven. Then let it cool afterwards.
