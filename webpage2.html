Cake In A Mug :)

  • Step 1: You will need a microwave safe 12 oz mug and a microwave.
  • Step 2: Put 3 teaspoons of flour, 3 tablespoons of sugar, 2 tablespoons of cocoa powder, 1/4 teaspoon of baking powder, and a pinch of salt into the mug.
  • Step 3: Blend well with a fork.
  • Step 4: Add 3 tablespoons of milk, 3 tablespoons of vegetable oil, and a splash of vanilla extract.
  • Step 5: Blend smoothly.
  • Step 6: Stir in 3 tablespoons of chocolate chips.
  • Step 7: Microwave on high for 90 seconds.
  • Step 8: Let it cool for 2 to 3 minutes before eating.
